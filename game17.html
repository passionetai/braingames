Game 17: Object Recall
What to Do
Gather 5 common objects (e.g., a cup, spoon, book, pen, apple) and place them on a table, or imagine them if you don’t have objects. Look at them for 1 minute, noting their details (e.g., color, shape). Cover them or look away for 1 minute, then recall and write down or say aloud as many of the 5 objects as you can. If it’s hard, picture a mental snapshot of the table!

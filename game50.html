Game 50: Guess Who
What to Do
Think of a person both you and your partner know, then describe them in 3–5 sentences without saying their name. Focus on traits like appearance or habits, and let your partner guess who it is before switching roles. Take 5–10 minutes to write your description below or share it aloud.

Game 13: Date Dash
What to Do
For each of the 5 dates below, think about what day of the week it was (e.g., Monday, Tuesday). Write the day next to each date in the space provided, or say it aloud. If you can’t recall, think of events around that time (e.g., “March 1 was near my birthday—maybe a Saturday?”). Take your time and enjoy the challenge!

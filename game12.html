Game 12: Name Game
What to Do
Think of 5 friends or family members and list their names below (first names are fine). Take 1 minute to review the list. Then, recall the names in reverse order and write them in the second set of lines. For example, if your list is Ann, Bob, Clara, Dan, Eve, the reverse is Eve, Dan, Clara, Bob, Ann.

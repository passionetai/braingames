Game 19: Picture Story
What to Do
Look at a picture for 1 minute (or imagine a park with a bench, a dog, a child playing, and a tree if no picture is available). Focus on 4 details (e.g., the dog’s color, what the child is doing). After 1 minute, look away, then write down or say aloud 4 details as a short story. If it’s tricky, close your eyes and picture the scene again!
